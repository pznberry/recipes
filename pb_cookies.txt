Ingredients:

2 cups peanut butter (creamy)
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans (optional)


Directions:

1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees F. Grease cookie sheet.

2. Combine peanut butter, eggs, and sugar and mix until smooth. Mix in nuts, if desired. Spoon dough by tablespoons onto a cookie sheet.

3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned. Let the cookies cool on the cookie sheets for 5 to 10 minutes before removing.
